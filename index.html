Welcome

This project is a simple sudoku game under the Java Beans convention.

Controls

To choose a field left click or navigate with keyboard arrows. Press a number to change the field value. If your choice is in conflict with pre-existing fields you will hear an alert sound and no change will be made. Otherwise field will change to the number of choice. Background of the field turns red when it is in conflict with other fields.
